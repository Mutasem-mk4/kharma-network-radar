Beyond Standard Monitoring

Kharma doesn't just watch your network. It actively hunts and neutralizes threats.

Active Defense (Auto-Kill)

Instantly terminates processes that connect to known malicious IPs or match flagged binary hashes via VirusTotal. The ultimate shield against data exfiltration.

Deep Packet Inspection

Real-time protocol detection and payload signature matching. Catches anomalies, unencrypted credentials, and suspicious byte-patterns mid-flight.

Live Radar & Geo-Fencing

A stunning web dashboard projecting connection routes globally. Automatically block entire countries or malicious ASNs with one click.

Intelligent Emulation Forensics

Pulls executables from memory, extracts strings, and performs heuristic analysis to uncover hidden intents before execution payloads trigger.

Multi-Node Swarm Hive

Federate multiple Kharma nodes across your infrastructure into a single pane of glass for true enterprise-level oversight.

Guardian Bot Alerts

Push critical breach alerts, DPI flags, and firewall blocks dynamically to Telegram or Discord as they happen.
